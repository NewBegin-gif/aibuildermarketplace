The Evolution of E-commerce: From Manual to Autonomous Systems

Historically, e-commerce has relied on manual processes, requiring human intervention to manage inventory, fulfill orders, and provide customer support. However, with the emergence of autonomous systems, e-commerce companies are now empowered to relinquish control to AI algorithms, enabling seamless customer experiences. For instance, companies like Amazon Go and Walmart's Grocery Pickup have already leveraged autonomous systems to revolutionize the way we shop. Amazon Go, for example, uses computer vision and machine learning to track items customers pick up, allowing for truly contactless shopping experiences.
