⚠️ Hidden Costs Founders MUST Know About

Smart founders budget for more than just the monthly fee. Be proactive in budgeting for these ancillary costs:

  • Withdrawal Fees and Network Gas: Bitvavo charges varying network fees (e.g., ETH, BTC) which are outside the subscription cost. Budget 1-3% of the transferred value for withdrawals.
  • KYC/AML Documentation Fees: While often waived, high initial corporate setup or complex multi-jurisdictional dealings may trigger documentation review fees.
  • OTC/High-Volume Trading Surcharges: Large, instantaneous, off-exchange volume transactions may incur a small, negotiated premium over standard market rates to manage risk.
  • Premium IP/Whitelist Fees: For Enterprise level compliance, the dedicated IP whitelisting setup typically includes a small annual setup fee.
