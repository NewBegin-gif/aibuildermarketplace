### 🖼️ Image Prompt (Afbeeldingsprompt voor Midjourney/DALL-E)
*(Minimalist, B2B, Sleek, Modern)*

**Prompt:** A minimalist, high-contrast conceptual image representing a strategic comparison between two sophisticated digital platforms. On the left side, feature a subtle, stylized graphical representation associated with finance/crypto (e.g., glowing blockchain nodes, structured data flow). On the right side, feature a cinematic, clean representation of video creation (e.g., interconnected timelines, playhead icon, professional camera lens). A central, subtle dividing line or a stylized 'VS' symbol should connect the two halves, symbolizing a strategic choice. Use a limited color palette of deep navy blue, professional teal, and crisp white/silver highlights. The overall aesthetic must be clean, architectural, and highly professional (B2B magazine cover style). Focus on negative space and conceptual connection rather than literal logos.
*(Style: Architectural visualization, photorealistic, clean gradients, minimalist, high detail, 16:9 aspect ratio)*
