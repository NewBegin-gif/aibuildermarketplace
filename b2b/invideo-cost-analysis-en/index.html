🛠️ What Each Plan Truly Includes (Feature Breakdown)

✅ Basic ($29/month):

Best for proof-of-concept content and limited testing. Great for single-asset needs.

  • Video Creation Credits: Low volume (Starter allocation).
  • Branding: Invideo watermark included.
  • Standard Templates: Access to core, non-premium assets.
  • Collaborators: None.
  • Usage: Personal or very small testing projects.

🥇 Pro ($59/month):

The core offering for most scaling founders. Provides significant creative control and volume.

  • Video Creation Credits: Increased allocation (Ideal for 1-2 campaigns/month).
  • Branding: Remove Invideo watermark (Crucial for professional content).
  • Assets: Premium Stock Footage & Music Library access.
  • Collaborators: Up to 3 team members.
  • Feature: Basic Workflow Integration (e.g., Zapier hookups).

🚀 Business ($99/month):

Designed for operational teams that require consistent, high-output content across multiple departments.

  • Video Creation Credits: High volume (Enterprise-ready bulk creation).
  • Branding: Full watermark removal.
  • Assets: Premium, unlimited stock library access.
  • Collaborators: Unlimited users and advanced permission settings.
  • Usage: Team dashboards, advanced brand kit management, API sandbox access.
  • Priority Support: Direct access to account manager.

🏢 Enterprise (Custom):

For large organizations with complex governance, security needs, and massive content scaling requirements.

  • Video Creation Credits: Negotiated, unlimited scope.
  • Branding: Full control, custom whitelabeling.
  • Integration: Dedicated API endpoints and SSO support.
  • Security: HIPAA, GDPR compliance options.
  • Support: Dedicated, 24/7 account management team.

💰 Hidden Costs Founders Must Budget For

⚠️ Advisory: Pricing guides are rarely complete. These hidden costs are where founders often get budget surprises. Budgeting for these proves you are a sophisticated buyer.

  • Usage Overages: While high-tier plans offer large credit pools, exceeding the monthly limits (e.g., running a major campaign unexpectedly) will incur credits-per-use fees, which can quickly add up.
  • Custom API Deployments: Integrating Invideo directly into your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot) requires professional API setup/consultation, which is usually billed hourly or via a setup fee.
  • Premium Human Touch Assets: While the template and asset libraries are vast, specific bespoke elements (e.g., deepfake voiceovers, specialized animators) may require third-party vendor paywalls.
  • Annual Contract Lock-in: To achieve the best discount, Founders committing to 12+ months are generally locked into that plan, requiring careful budget predictions for the full year.

⚖️ The Founder's Verdict: Choosing Your Plan

Do not purchase based only on the lowest monthly cost. Purchase based on the *biggest bottleneck* in your current marketing process.

🏆 Recommended Plan: Pro ($59/year).
