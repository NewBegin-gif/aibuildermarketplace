### 🖼️ Image Prompt (Midjourney / DALL-E Prompt)
**(Focus: Minimalist, Abstract Representation of Two Different Tech Disciplines in Harmony)**

**Prompt:**
A highly stylized, minimalist, and abstract digital composition representing the intersection of visual media and coding. Depict two distinct but complementary energy flows: one flow should represent fluid, dynamic creativity (like wave patterns or light gradients, symbolizing video/design), and the other should represent structured, precise logic (like intricate, glowing circuit boards or binary code pathways, symbolizing development/coding). The two flows should meet or pass through a central, clean nexus point. Use a muted, sophisticated color palette: deep navy blue, charcoal gray, pops of electric cyan, and subtle gold accents. The overall aesthetic must be ultra-modern, B2B, clean, and sophisticated. **Shot on: High-resolution digital art. Aspect Ratio: 2:3 (Pinterest Vertical).**

***

### ✨ Rationale Notes (For implementation)

*   **B2B Aesthetic:** Avoiding literal screenshots ensures the pin looks like high-level strategy content, not just a simple comparison article.
*   **Visual Metaphor:** The prompt doesn't show "InVideo" or "Replit"; it shows the *concept* of the choice (Creativity vs. Logic), which is more engaging and abstractly appealing for a Pinterest feed.
*   **Tone:** The title uses the word "potenciará" (will power/enhance) to give a sense of strong, actionable outcome, appealing to business users.
