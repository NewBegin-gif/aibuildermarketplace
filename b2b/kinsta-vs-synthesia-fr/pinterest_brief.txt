Votre stratégie digitale doit être solide : de l’infrastructure parfaite à la création de contenu percutant. 🤔 Kinsta excelle dans la fondation, tandis que Synthesia révolutionne la communication vidéo. Notre comparatif vous aide à déterminer si vous devez d'abord améliorer votre hébergement ou votre capacité à produire du contenu IA professionnel. Lisez pour choisir le bon investissement ! 👇

### 🖼️ Image Prompt (Midjourney / DALL-E)

**Style:** Ultra-minimalist, B2B corporate, architectural.
**Vibe:** Clean, sophisticated, future-forward, conceptual.

**Prompt:**
A conceptual, minimalist composition on a pure white/off-white background. The image must visually represent the dichotomy between *stable infrastructure* (like code/hosting) and *dynamic, fluid content* (like video/AI).

On the left side, depict a perfectly structured, glowing geometric grid or schematic representing secure code, data stacks, and stability (Kinsta concept). The grid should be rendered in deep Prussian blue or charcoal grey.

On the right side, depict a translucent, flowing sheet of light or a sophisticated, cinematic liquid abstraction that suggests motion, media, and intelligence (Synthesia concept). This side should be colored warm gold, electric blue, and subtle cyan.

The two elements (structure and flow) should meet in the center, not fighting, but merging into a balanced, glowing point. Above this merging point, place a subtle, abstract magnifying glass or directional arrow pointing upwards, symbolizing growth.

**Technical parameters:** *Minimalist, highly detailed, 3D rendering, architectural concept, clean lines, negative space, cinematic lighting. Aspect Ratio: 2:3*
