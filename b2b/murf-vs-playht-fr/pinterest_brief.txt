# 🇫🇷 Pinterest Pin Brief: Murf vs Play.ht

This brief is designed to appeal to digital marketers, content creators, and small business owners seeking professional voice-over solutions.

***

### 🎯 Goal
To drive organic traffic to a comparative article analyzing the strengths and weaknesses of Murf and Play.ht for high-stakes commercial use.

### ✨ Pinterest Title (Max 60 characters)
**Quelle voix IA choisir pour votre contenu ? Murf vs Play.ht**

### 📝 Description copy
Ne perdez plus de temps à choisir. Nous avons analysé en profondeur Murf et Play.ht pour que vous n'ayez pas à le faire ! 🔊 Découvrez quelle technologie de voix IA correspond *précisément* aux besoins et au budget de votre marque. Cliquez pour décoder le meilleur choix.

#VoixIA #MarketingDigital #CréationDeContenu

### 🎨 Image Prompt for Midjourney / DALL-E

**Style:** Minimalist, Flat Design, B2B Aesthetic.
**Composition:** A clean, visually striking comparison.
**Concept:** Feature two distinct, modern platforms (representing the two competitors) connected by a clear decision point (a question mark or a scale).

**The Prompt:**
> Minimalist vector illustration comparing two advanced AI platforms. The aesthetic must be clean, digital, and corporate (B2B). Use a limited, sophisticated color palette: deep slate blue, muted teal, crisp white, and subtle pops of gold/orange for emphasis. On the left, represent "Murf" with a clean, structured vertical element. On the right, represent "Play.ht" with a slightly more flowing, organic element. Place a stylized, glowing sound wave infographic in the center, connecting the two sides and pointing toward a clear choice graphic (like a simplified balance scale or arrow).
