🔍 Detailed Feature Breakdown: What Each Plan Guarantees

Synthesia's pricing structure moves beyond simple credits; it determines the sophistication (security, API access) and scale (user count, video volume) of your deployment. Understanding the line between 'good enough' and 'mission-critical' is key.

🟢 Free Tier Limitations (Proof-of-Concept)

  • ✅ What Works: Basic video creation, limited access to template library, watermarking (a key limitation), low video resolution (SD quality).
  • ❌ What Doesn't: No API integration; no bulk credits; limited user seats (usually 1); inability to upload custom brand assets or use advanced security features (SSO).
  • 💡 Founder Insight: Use this only for concept validation. Do not rely on it for client deliverables.

⭐️ Starter Plan (Budget Focus)

  • Capacity: Up to 5 users.
  • Video Credits: Approx. 50 minutes of video generation per month.
  • Key Features: Basic branding, standard avatars, simple editing suite.
  • Cost Benefit: Enough for internal training or very small marketing initiatives.

🚀 Pro Plan (The Startup Sweet Spot)

  • Capacity: Up to 25 users.
  • Video Credits: High allocation (150-200+ minutes) + Credit Boosts.
  • Key Features: API Access (Critical), Custom Domain Branding, Advanced Avatar Voice Cloning (limited minutes), Multi-user collaboration history.
  • Recommended Use: SaaS companies, Mid-sized marketing agencies needing automation.

🏢 Enterprise Plan (Scale & Security)

  • Capacity: Unlimited or negotiated user count.
  • Video Credits: Unlimited (or extremely high) dedicated capacity.
  • Key Features: On-Premise/Private Cloud Deployment Options, SSO/SAML Integration, Custom Compliance Auditing, Dedicated Account Manager, Custom API Extensions.
  • Cost Justification: The cost is not just for video; it's for guaranteed uptime, compliance, and customized pipeline integration.

💰 Hidden Costs Analysis: What the Billing Invoice Won't Tell You

The quoted monthly fee ($X/month) is rarely the total cost. Startups must account for operational overhead. These hidden costs are the primary reason plans unexpectedly run over budget.

  1. Overage Fees on Credits: This is the biggest trap. If you exceed your monthly credit allotment (e.g., going over 200 minutes), Synthesia charges a tiered supplemental rate (e.g., $0.15 - $0.25 per minute).

    Cost Management Tip: Underestimate your video needs by 30% and purchase an extra 20% of credits, rather than facing high overage fees mid-month.

  2. Dedicated Integration/Setup Fees: For Enterprise users, the cost of integrating Synthesia's API into your internal CRM or LMS system is separate. Expect one-time setup fees ranging from $1,500 to $5,000, depending on complexity.
  3. Premium Asset Licensure: Some premium voices, custom background footage, or advanced avatar character licenses are billed add-on, even on the Pro Plan. Always check the per-asset cost.
